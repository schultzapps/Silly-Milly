## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I add my own photos?**
Tap the **+** button on the home screen to create a new collection. From there you can add photos from your photo library, give each one a name, and start playing right away.

**How do I change the card display mode?**
Go to **Settings** (the ••• button on the home screen) and tap **Card Display**. You can choose between photo only, photo + name, or name only.

**How do I play with multiplayer?**
Tap the gear icon on the home screen to open Game Settings, then enable **2, 3, or 4 Players**. Players take turns — a matched pair keeps your turn, a miss passes it to the other player.

**My photos aren't syncing to another device.**
Make sure both devices are signed into the same iCloud account and have iCloud enabled. Sync is handled automatically by Apple's iCloud — it may take a few minutes to appear on another device.

**How do I reset my scores?**
Go to **Settings** and tap **Reset Scores**. This clears best times, best taps, and games played for all collections. Your photos and collections are not affected.

**How do I hide the Milly starter collection?**
Go to **Settings** and toggle off **Show Milly's Collection**. You can turn it back on at any time. (This option is only available once you've created at least one collection of your own.)

**Can I delete the starter collection?**
The Milly starter collection can be hidden but not deleted — it's built into the app. Hide it in Settings if you'd prefer not to see it.

**How do I restore a tip purchase?**
Tips are consumable purchases and cannot be restored — that's just how consumable in-app purchases work on the App Store. If you have a billing question, please contact Apple Support directly.

**The app isn't working as expected.**
Please email us at [schultzapps@gmail.com](mailto:schultzapps@gmail.com) and describe what's happening. Screenshots are always helpful!
